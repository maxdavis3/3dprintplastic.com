CAPABILITIES

Plastic Printing Processes

01

Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)

Nylon PA12 and PA11 SLS for functional, isotropic parts without support structures. MJF and SLS for complex assemblies, hinges, and lattice structures in a single build. Production quantities with consistent mechanical properties.

02

FDM Engineering Polymers

PEEK, PEKK, Ultem 9085, ASA, and PC-ABS for high-temperature and chemical-resistant applications. Aerospace-grade materials with certified material properties. Large-format FDM for jigs, fixtures, and tooling.

03

PolyJet Multi-Material

Simultaneous rigid-rubber multi-material printing for overmold simulation, gaskets, and ergonomic grips. Shore hardness from 27A to rigid clear in a single build. Layer resolution 16µm for smooth optical surfaces.
